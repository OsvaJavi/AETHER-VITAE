About Aether Vitae

Our Mission

Aether Vitae is a conceptual project born at the intersection of astrobiology and artificial intelligence. Our mission is to inspire curiosity and accelerate research into the possibilities of life beyond Earth by providing innovative tools and a collaborative platform for both scientists and enthusiasts.

The Project

This portal serves as the gateway to our two primary initiatives. The 'Scientist' path leads to our proprietary Large Language Model, trained on a vast corpus of astrobiological data, designed to assist researchers in formulating hypotheses and analyzing data. The 'Space Explorer' path will guide users through an immersive, educational journey about the conditions for life in our cosmos.
